Essential Security Tips

At Bangko Filipino, your security is our top priority. Follow these proven practices to protect your accounts and personal information from potential threats.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Activate 2FA in Profile Management to add an extra layer of security for every login. Two-factor authentication ensures that even if someone knows your password, they can't access your account without the second verification method.

Beware of Phishing

We will never ask for your full password or OTP by email or phone. Always verify links before clicking. When in doubt, access our website directly by typing the URL in your browser rather than clicking on links in emails or text messages.

Secure Your Device

Keep your computer and mobile OS up to date, install antivirus software, and avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. Enable automatic updates to ensure you have the latest security patches protecting your devices.
