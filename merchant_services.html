Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get approved for merchant services?
Most applications are processed within 1-2 business days. Once approved, you can typically start accepting payments within 3-5 business days, depending on your chosen solution and equipment needs.
Can I accept international payments?
Yes, our merchant services allow you to accept payments in multiple currencies from customers around the world. Additional fees may apply for currency conversion and international transactions.
What types of businesses do you serve?
We provide merchant services to a wide range of businesses, from small retail shops to large enterprises across various industries including retail, restaurants, healthcare, professional services, e-commerce, and more.
How do I integrate payment processing with my existing website?
Our payment gateway offers easy integration with most popular e-commerce platforms and shopping carts. We also provide APIs and developer resources for custom integrations. Our technical support team is available to assist you throughout the integration process.
What security measures are in place to protect transactions?
We employ industry-leading security measures including PCI DSS compliance, point-to-point encryption, tokenization, fraud detection tools, and EMV chip technology to protect both your business and your customers' sensitive information.
