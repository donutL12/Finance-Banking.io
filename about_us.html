About Bangko Filipino

Your trusted financial partner committed to excellence and innovation since 1998

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to foster financial inclusion and empower our clients to achieve their financial goals through accessible, reliable, and personalized services that address their unique needs.

We envision a future where every Filipino has access to world-class banking services that enable economic growth, financial security, and prosperity for individuals, families, and businesses across the nation.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations and interactions, ensuring transparency and trustworthiness.

Innovation

We continuously seek new and better ways to serve our customers through technological advancements and creative solutions.

Inclusion

We are committed to making financial services accessible to all Filipinos, regardless of their background or economic status.

Our History

Since our establishment in 1998, Bangko Filipino has grown from a single branch in Manila to a nationwide network, serving thousands of customers with integrity and excellence.

1998

Bangko Filipino was founded with a single branch in Manila, with a vision to provide accessible banking to all Filipinos.

2005

Expanded to 25 branches across major cities in Luzon, offering a wider range of financial services.

2012

Launched our first digital banking platform, revolutionizing how our customers access financial services.

2018

Celebrated our 20th anniversary with the opening of our 100th branch and introduction of our award-winning mobile app.

2025

Today, we continue to grow and innovate, serving millions of Filipinos with comprehensive banking solutions nationwide.

Leadership Team

Our bank is led by a team of experienced professionals with decades of combined experience in the banking and financial sectors. Their vision and guidance have shaped Bangko Filipino into the trusted institution it is today.

Each member of our leadership team brings unique expertise and perspectives, united by a shared commitment to our values and mission. Together, they ensure that Bangko Filipino continues to provide exceptional service while adapting to the evolving financial landscape.
