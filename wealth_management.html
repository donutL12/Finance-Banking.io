Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment required for wealth management services?
Our Premier tier wealth management services start at ‚Ç±2 million in investable assets. However, we also offer investment advisory services for clients with smaller portfolios. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
How are wealth management fees structured?
Our fee structure varies based on the tier of service and portfolio size. We offer both asset-based fees and flat fee options. During your initial consultation, we'll provide complete transparency on all fees associated with your wealth management plan.
Can I transfer existing investments to Bangko Filipino?
Yes, we have a seamless process for transferring existing investments. Our team will work with you to evaluate your current portfolio and develop a transition strategy that minimizes disruption and potential tax implications.
How often will I meet with my wealth advisor?
Meeting frequency varies by service tier and individual preferences. Premier clients typically have quarterly reviews, Elite clients have monthly reviews, and Private clients have on-demand access to their advisory team. We also schedule additional meetings around significant life events or major market changes.
What makes Bangko Filipino's wealth management different?
Our approach combines global expertise with local market knowledge. We focus on building long-term relationships through personalized service, transparent communication, and comprehensive wealth strategies that consider all aspects of your financial life and future generations.
