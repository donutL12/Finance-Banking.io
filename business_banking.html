Business Banking FAQs

What types of business accounts do you offer?
We offer a wide range of business accounts including business checking, business savings, merchant services accounts, and specialized accounts for different industries and business sizes. Each account type comes with features tailored to specific business needs.
What documents do I need to open a business account?
Required documents typically include business registration certificates, tax identification numbers, valid ID of business owners/signatories, proof of business address, and business financial statements. Requirements may vary based on business type and structure.
Do you offer financing options for small businesses?
Yes, we offer various financing solutions for small businesses including business loans, lines of credit, equipment financing, and SBA-backed loans. Our relationship managers can help determine the best options for your specific business needs.
How can I apply for merchant services?
You can apply for merchant services online through our business banking portal, by visiting any of our branches, or by contacting our business services team. We'll guide you through the application process and help set up payment processing solutions tailored to your business.
What treasury management services do you provide?
Our treasury management services include cash management, electronic payments and collections, fraud prevention tools, liquidity management, and investment services. These solutions are designed to help businesses optimize cash flow and financial operations.
