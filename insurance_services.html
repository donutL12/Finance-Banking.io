Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file an insurance claim? +

To file a claim, you can either log into your account on our online banking portal, call our dedicated claims hotline at 1-800-BANGKO, or visit any of our branch locations. Our claims team will guide you through the process and documentation requirements.

Can I customize my insurance coverage? +

Yes, all our insurance plans come with flexible options that can be tailored to your specific needs. Our insurance advisors can help you select the right coverage options and adjust protection levels based on your circumstances and budget.

How quickly are claims typically processed? +

Most standard claims are processed within 5-7 business days once all required documentation has been received. For emergency claims, we offer an expedited process that can provide benefits within 24-48 hours in qualifying situations.

Is there a waiting period for new insurance policies? +

Waiting periods vary by insurance type. Health insurance typically has a 30-day waiting period for non-emergency care, while life insurance policies generally become effective immediately after the first premium payment. Property insurance usually becomes active within 24 hours of policy issuance.

Can I manage my insurance policies online? +

Yes, all insurance policies can be managed through our secure online banking portal. You can view policy details, make premium payments, adjust coverage, add beneficiaries, and file claims all from one convenient dashboard.
