Home Loan Agreement Terms

By submitting this application, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

  1. I/We certify that all information provided in this application is true, complete, and correct.
  2. I/We authorize Bangko Filipino to verify any information provided in this application from any source.
  3. I/We understand that submission of this application does not guarantee approval of the loan.
  4. I/We acknowledge that Bangko Filipino may request additional documentation to process this application.
  5. I/We understand that if approved, the loan will be subject to the terms and conditions specified in the final loan agreement.
  6. I/We consent to Bangko Filipino collecting, using, and disclosing my/our personal information for the purposes of processing this application and administering the loan.

Why Choose Our Home Loan?

  • Competitive interest rates starting at 5.25% p.a.
  • Flexible loan terms up to 30 years
  • Finance up to 80% of property value
  • Simplified application process
  • Dedicated loan officer to guide you

Application Requirements

To ensure fast processing of your loan, please have the following documents ready:

  • Valid government ID (Passport, Driver's License, etc.)
  • Proof of income (Latest 3 months payslips, ITR)
  • Certificate of Employment
  • Latest 3 months bank statements
  • Property documents (if available)

Next Steps

After submitting your application:

  1. Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days
  2. You'll receive an email notification about your pre-approval status
  3. If pre-approved, a loan officer will contact you for document verification
  4. Property appraisal will be scheduled
  5. Final approval and loan disbursement
