Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount required?
Our standard portfolio management service requires a minimum investment of ‚Ç±1,000,000. However, we also offer specialized services for different investment levels. Please contact our advisors for more information.
How are your fees structured?
We use a transparent fee structure based on a percentage of assets under management, typically ranging from 0.75% to 1.5% annually, depending on portfolio size and complexity. This ensures our interests are aligned with growing your assets.
How frequently will my portfolio be reviewed?
Your portfolio is continuously monitored by our team. Formal reviews occur quarterly, with performance reports sent to you. However, we make tactical adjustments whenever market conditions or your circumstances require it.
Can I access my funds if needed?
Yes, your investments remain liquid and accessible. While we recommend maintaining your investment strategy for optimal long-term results, you can request withdrawals at any time with no penalties from Bangko Filipino.
What types of investments do you use in portfolios?
We create diversified portfolios using a mix of asset classes including equities, fixed income, alternative investments, and cash equivalents. The specific allocation depends on your individual goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon.
