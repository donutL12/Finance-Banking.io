What types of accounts can I open?
Bangko Filipino offers a variety of accounts to meet your financial needs:
  • Savings Accounts: Interest-bearing accounts for building your savings
  • Checking Accounts: For everyday transactions with debit card access
  • Business Accounts: Tailored solutions for businesses of all sizes
  • Time Deposit Accounts: Fixed-term savings with higher interest rates
  • Foreign Currency Accounts: Hold and transact in multiple currencies

Visit our Personal Banking or Business Banking pages for more details on each account type.

How do I enroll in online banking?

Enrolling in our online banking service is quick and easy:

  1. Click on the "Online Banking" dropdown in the header navigation
  2. Select "Sign Up" from the dropdown menu
  3. Enter your account number and other required personal information
  4. Create a username and secure password
  5. Set up your security questions and answers
  6. Verify your identity through the one-time password sent to your registered mobile number

Once registered, you can access your accounts 24/7, transfer funds, pay bills, and manage your finances from anywhere.

Can I apply for a loan online?

Yes, you can apply for various loan products through our online platform:

  • Personal loans
  • Home loans and mortgages
  • Auto loans
  • Business loans
  • Equipment financing

Visit our Loans & Mortgages page to start your application. You'll need to provide documentation including proof of income, identification, and other relevant information depending on the loan type. Our loan specialists will review your application and contact you within 1-2 business days.

What are the requirements to open an account?

To open an account with Bangko Filipino, you'll need:

  • Valid ID: Government-issued identification (passport, driver's license, etc.)
  • Proof of Address: Recent utility bill, lease agreement, or similar document
  • TIN: Tax Identification Number
  • Initial Deposit: Minimum opening balance varies by account type

For business accounts, additional requirements include business registration documents, business permits, and articles of incorporation (for corporations).

How can I reset my online banking password?

If you forgot your password or need to reset it:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password" link below the login fields
  3. Enter your username and the captcha code
  4. Select your preferred verification method (email or SMS)
  5. Enter the verification code sent to your registered email or mobile number
  6. Create and confirm your new password

For security reasons, your new password must contain at least 8 characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

What are your banking hours?

Branch Banking Hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM (select branches only)
  • Sunday and Holidays: Closed

ATM and Online Banking:

Our ATM network and online banking services are available 24/7, allowing you to conduct most banking transactions anytime, anywhere.

Please note that branch hours may vary during holidays or special circumstances. Check our Branch Locator for specific branch details.
