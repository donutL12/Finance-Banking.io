Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about retirement planning and our services.

When should I start planning for retirement?
The best time to start planning for retirement is as early as possible. The power of compound interest means that the earlier you begin saving, the less you'll need to contribute overall to reach your retirement goals. Ideally, you should start in your 20s or as soon as you begin your career, but it's never too late to start.
How much money will I need for retirement?
The amount you'll need for retirement depends on several factors, including your desired lifestyle, expected lifespan, healthcare needs, and inflation. A common guideline is to aim for 70-80% of your pre-retirement income. Our retirement calculator can help you estimate your specific needs, and our advisors can provide a more detailed analysis.
What retirement accounts does Bangko Filipino offer?
Bangko Filipino offers various retirement account options, including Personal Pension Plans, Time Deposit Retirement Accounts, Mutual Fund Retirement Portfolios, and Variable Universal Life Insurance with investment components. Each has different features, benefits, and tax implications. Our advisors can help determine which options best suit your needs.
How often should I review my retirement plan?
We recommend reviewing your retirement plan at least annually and after major life events such as marriage, birth of a child, career change, or inheritance. Regular reviews ensure your plan remains aligned with your goals and adapts to changing market conditions or personal circumstances.
Can I access my retirement savings before retirement?
While it's generally advisable to keep your retirement savings untouched until retirement, certain retirement accounts do offer flexibility for early withdrawals. However, early withdrawals may incur penalties or tax consequences. Our advisors can discuss specific options that provide the balance of growth and accessibility that meets your needs.
