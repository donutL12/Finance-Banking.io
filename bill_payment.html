Frequently Asked Questions

When will my payment be processed?
Payments made before 3:00 PM on business days are typically processed on the same day. Payments made after the cut-off time or on weekends and holidays will be processed on the next business day.
Is there a fee for using the bill payment service?
No, Bangko Filipino does not charge any fees for using our online bill payment service. However, some billers may charge a convenience fee for processing payments, which will be disclosed before you confirm your payment.
How do I set up automatic payments?
To set up automatic payments, select your biller, enter your account information, and choose the "Set Up Automatic Payment" option. You can specify payment amounts, frequency, and start/end dates according to your preferences.
