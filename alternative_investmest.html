Alternative Investments

Diversify your portfolio beyond traditional assets with our exclusive alternative investment options

Explore Alternative Investments

Alternative investments represent assets that fall outside the conventional investment categories of stocks, bonds, and cash. At Bangko Filipino, we offer access to a diverse range of alternative investment vehicles designed to enhance portfolio diversification, provide hedging against market volatility, and potentially generate higher returns.

Our team of experienced investment professionals will guide you through the selection process, ensuring that your alternative investment choices align with your financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment timeline.

Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)

Gain exposure to income-generating real estate without directly owning property.

  • Stable income through regular dividend distributions
  • Diversification across various property types
  • Lower minimum investment compared to direct property ownership
  • High liquidity compared to physical real estate

Private Equity Funds

Invest in privately-held companies with high growth potential not available on public markets.

  • Access to exclusive investment opportunities
  • Potential for above-market returns
  • Professional management by experienced fund managers
  • Diversification across multiple private companies

Commodities & Precious Metals

Add tangible assets to your portfolio with investments in gold, silver, agricultural products, and more.

  • Hedge against inflation and currency fluctuations
  • Low correlation with traditional financial markets
  • Option for physical ownership or paper investments
  • Historical store of value during economic uncertainty

Hedge Funds

Access sophisticated investment strategies managed by elite fund managers with flexible approaches.

  • Potential for absolute returns regardless of market direction
  • Diverse strategies including long/short, global macro, and more
  • Active risk management techniques
  • Lower correlation with traditional market movements

Sustainable & Impact Investments

Generate positive social and environmental impact alongside financial returns through sustainable investments.

  • Support environmentally and socially responsible companies
  • Access emerging trends in renewable energy and clean technology
  • Make a positive difference while seeking competitive returns
  • Alignment with UN Sustainable Development Goals

Art & Collectibles

Invest in fine art, rare collectibles, and other tangible assets with appreciation potential.

  • Access to curated art and collectible opportunities
  • Professional authentication and valuation services
  • Portfolio diversification through unique assets
  • Potential for substantial long-term appreciation

Benefits of Alternative Investments

Portfolio Diversification

Alternative investments typically have low correlation with traditional asset classes, helping to reduce overall portfolio volatility and enhance risk-adjusted returns.

Inflation Hedge

Many alternative investments, particularly real assets like real estate and commodities, have historically served as effective hedges against inflation, preserving purchasing power.

Enhanced Return Potential

Alternative investments can offer higher return potential compared to traditional investments, especially in inefficient markets where skilled managers can identify overlooked opportunities.

Income Generation

Many alternative investments, such as REITs and certain private debt instruments, can provide steady income streams, which is particularly valuable in low-yield environments.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount for alternative investments?

Minimum investment amounts vary by product. Our REITs and commodity-based investments typically start at ‚Ç±100,000, while private equity funds and hedge funds generally require a minimum of ‚Ç±1,000,000. We also offer certain fund-of-funds products that provide access to alternative investments with lower minimums.

Are alternative investments suitable for all investors?

Alternative investments typically have higher risk profiles and may not be suitable for all investors. They often work best as part of a diversified portfolio for investors with longer investment horizons and higher risk tolerance. Our advisors will help determine if these investments align with your financial situation and goals.

How liquid are alternative investments?

Liquidity varies widely among alternative investments. Publicly traded REITs offer high liquidity similar to stocks, while private equity funds typically have lockup periods of several years. We'll clearly explain the liquidity terms of each investment option before you commit your capital.

What fees are associated with alternative investments?

Fee structures for alternative investments typically include management fees (ranging from 1-2% annually) and may include performance fees (typically 10-20% of profits above a certain threshold). We prioritize transparency and will provide a comprehensive fee disclosure for any investment you consider.

How are alternative investments taxed?

Tax treatment varies by investment type and structure. Some investments may offer tax advantages, while others might have complex tax reporting requirements. Our team works with tax professionals to help you understand the potential tax implications of your investment choices.
