How It Works

Sending money internationally has never been easier. Our streamlined process ensures your funds reach their destination securely and efficiently.

1

Prepare Your Information

Gather recipient details including their full name, bank name, account number, and SWIFT/BIC code. For some countries, additional routing information like IBAN or sort codes may be required.

2

Initiate Your Transfer

Log in to your Bangko Filipino online banking portal, select International Wire Transfer, and enter the recipient details and transfer amount. You can also visit any branch or call our International Banking team for assistance.

3

Verify all details including the exchange rate, fees, and estimated delivery time. For transfers over certain thresholds, additional verification may be required for your security.

Monitor the status of your transfer through our online banking platform, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service. You'll receive notifications at key milestones of the transfer process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our international wire transfer services.

How long does an international wire transfer take?

Standard international wire transfers typically take 1-3 business days to complete, depending on the destination country, intermediary banks involved, and local banking hours. Express transfers can be completed within 24 hours for select destinations, while premium services may offer same-day delivery for certain major financial centers.

What information do I need to make an international wire transfer?

To make an international wire transfer, you'll need:

  • Recipient's full legal name as it appears on their bank account
  • Recipient's complete bank account number or IBAN
  • Recipient bank's name, address, and SWIFT/BIC code
  • For some countries, additional routing information such as sort codes, BSB, or branch numbers
  • Purpose of payment
Are there limits on how much money I can transfer internationally?

Yes, there are daily and monthly limits for international wire transfers based on your account type and relationship with the bank. Standard accounts typically have a daily limit of ‚Ç±500,000 and a monthly limit of ‚Ç±2,000,000. Premium and business accounts have higher limits. For large transfers, additional verification and documentation may be required to comply with regulatory requirements.

Can I cancel or recall an international wire transfer?

Wire transfers can sometimes be canceled if they haven't been processed yet. Contact our customer service immediately if you need to cancel a transfer. However, once a wire transfer has been processed and sent to the beneficiary bank, it usually cannot be recalled without the recipient's consent. Cancellation requests may incur additional fees, and there's no guarantee that funds can be recovered.

How secure are international wire transfers?

Bangko Filipino employs bank-grade security measures for all international wire transfers, including advanced encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. We also follow strict verification procedures to confirm the identity of senders and validate recipient information. Additionally, all international transfers are processed through the SWIFT network, which maintains rigorous security standards for global financial messaging.
