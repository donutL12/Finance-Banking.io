Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to open a business account?

For most businesses, you can complete the application process online in about 15-20 minutes. Once we've received all required documentation, your account can typically be opened within 2-3 business days. For more complex business structures, additional time may be required for verification.

What documents do I need to open a business account?

Required documents typically include:

  • Business registration or incorporation documents
  • Business Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • Valid government-issued ID for all signatories
  • Business address verification
  • Business license (if applicable)

Additional documentation may be required depending on your business type and structure.

Can I open an account for a newly formed business?

Yes, we welcome newly formed businesses. You'll need to provide your business registration documents and other standard requirements. For startups less than six months old, we may require additional information about your business plan and projected finances.

How do I switch my business accounts to Bangko Filipino?

Our Switch Specialist team makes changing banks easy. Once your new account is open, we'll help you:

  • Transfer automatic payments and deposits
  • Set up your online banking and bill payments
  • Provide switch notification letters for your vendors
  • Close your old accounts when you're ready

We aim to make the transition as smooth as possible with minimal disruption to your business operations.

Are business accounts PDIC insured?

Yes, all Bangko Filipino business deposit accounts are insured by the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) up to ‚Ç±500,000 per depositor. This insurance provides an extra layer of security for your business funds.
