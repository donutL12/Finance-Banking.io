Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mutual fund offerings.

What is the minimum investment amount? ‚ñº

The minimum initial investment varies by fund type. For most funds, the minimum initial investment is ‚Ç±5,000, with subsequent investments of ‚Ç±1,000. Special funds may require higher minimums. Please check the specific fund details for exact requirements.

How can I redeem my investment? ‚ñº

You can redeem your investment by submitting a redemption request through our online banking portal or by visiting any Bangko Filipino branch. Redemption proceeds are typically credited to your designated account within 3-5 business days after receiving your request.

Are there fees associated with mutual fund investments? ‚ñº

Yes, our mutual funds have associated fees that include management fees (typically 1.0% to 2.5% per annum depending on the fund), early redemption fees (if applicable), and front-end or back-end loads (if applicable). These fees are clearly disclosed in each fund's prospectus.

Can I switch between different mutual funds? ‚ñº

Yes, we offer the flexibility to switch between different mutual funds. You can do this through our online platform or by submitting a fund switch form. Some funds may have specific holding period requirements or switching fees, so please consult with an investment advisor before making a switch.

How are my investments taxed? ‚ñº

Mutual fund investments are subject to applicable taxes based on current tax regulations. Generally, capital gains and dividends from mutual funds are subject to income tax. For specific tax implications based on your personal situation, we recommend consulting with a tax advisor.
