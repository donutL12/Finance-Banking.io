Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about investing with Bangko Filipino

What is the minimum amount I need to start investing?
The minimum investment amount varies by product. Time deposits start at ‚Ç±5,000, mutual funds at ‚Ç±5,000, and UITFs at ‚Ç±10,000. We believe everyone should have access to investment opportunities, so we've kept our minimums accessible to help you start your investment journey.
How do I monitor my investments?
You can monitor your investments through our online banking portal, mobile app, or by visiting any Bangko Filipino branch. For mutual funds and UITFs, we provide regular statements and daily NAV updates. Our relationship managers are also available to help you understand your portfolio performance.
Can I withdraw my investment before maturity?
Yes, most of our investment products offer liquidity options. Time deposits may incur penalties for early withdrawal. Mutual funds and UITFs can be redeemed on business days, though the redemption price depends on the NAV at the time of redemption. We recommend discussing your liquidity needs with our advisors before investing.
Are my investments insured?
Time deposits are covered by the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) up to ‚Ç±500,000 per depositor. Mutual funds and UITFs are not deposit products and are not insured by PDIC. These are investment products where returns are not guaranteed and principal may be at risk depending on market conditions.
How are investment returns taxed?
Interest income from time deposits is subject to a final tax of 20%. For mutual funds and UITFs, tax treatment depends on the fund type and holding period. Equity funds held for more than 5 years may be exempt from capital gains tax. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.
